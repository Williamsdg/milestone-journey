Privacy Policy

Milestone Journey
Last Updated: December 30, 2024

Our Commitment: Your family's privacy is our top priority. We collect only what's necessary to provide the app's functionality, and we never sell or share your personal data with third parties.

1. Information We Collect

Milestone Journey collects the following information to provide our baby tracking services:

  • Account Information: Email address and password for authentication
  • Child Profiles: First name, last name, and gender of your children
  • Feeding Logs: Bottle feeding times, amounts, milk type, and related notes
  • Diaper/Potty Logs: Change times, types, and related notes
  • Medicine Logs: Medicine names, dosages, times administered, and reminder settings
  • Doctor Information: Pediatrician name, office details, and contact information you choose to save

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Provide and maintain the Milestone Journey app
  • Display your tracked data back to you
  • Generate daily reports for your personal use
  • Send medicine reminder notifications (if enabled)
  • Sync your data across your devices

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is securely stored using Google Firebase, a cloud platform that provides:

  • Industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication through Firebase Authentication
  • Data isolation ensuring only you can access your family's information
  • Regular security audits and compliance certifications

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties.

Your data is only accessible by:

  • You (the account holder)
  • Firebase/Google Cloud (our infrastructure provider, bound by their privacy policies)

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data associated with your account within the app
  • Delete: Remove individual entries or delete your entire account
  • Export: Generate PDF reports of your data
  • Correct: Edit any information you've entered

6. Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time through the app's Settings screen. When you delete your account:

  • All your personal data is permanently removed
  • All children profiles and associated entries are deleted
  • This action cannot be undone

7. Children's Privacy

Milestone Journey is designed for parents and caregivers to track information about their children. We do not knowingly collect information directly from children under 13. All data is entered and controlled by the parent or caregiver account holder.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
